About SeeInsta

SeeInsta is a fast and reliable platform for downloading Instagram videos, reels, and stories with ease. Simply paste the URL and download your desired content instantly.

How to Use SeeInsta

1. Copy the Instagram video, reel, or story URL.

2. Paste the URL into the input field on seeinsta.com.

3. Click the "Download" button to save the content to your device.

How to Delete Instagram

Deleting your Instagram account is a straightforward process. Follow these steps:

1. Open a browser (on your phone or computer) and go to the Instagram Account Deletion Page.

2. Log in to your Instagram account if you're not already logged in.

3. Choose a reason from the dropdown menu for why you're deleting your account.

4. Re-enter your password to confirm the account deletion request.

5. Finally, click the "Delete [Your Account Name]" button to permanently remove your account. Note: Your account will be deactivated immediately, but it will take 30 days to be permanently deleted. During this time, your account will be invisible, and you can log in again to cancel the deletion.
